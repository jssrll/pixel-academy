Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my purchased books?
After purchase, your books will appear in "My Library" page. You can access them anytime, anywhere.
How do I get student discounts?
Go to the Featured page and enter your student code to get exclusive discounts and credits.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept GCash and Cash payments at our campus office.
Can I share my purchased books with classmates?
Each purchase is for individual use. Please encourage classmates to get their own copies to support the platform.
How do I invest my learning credits?
Go to the Featured page and choose between Savings Plan Option 1 (3% in 90 days) or Option 2 (6% in 150 days).
How do I create a student account?
Click the student icon, select "Create Account", fill in your details using your Minsu student email or phone number.
How do I contact support?
You can reach us through Live Chat on our website or email us at pixelacademy@minsu.edu.ph
Are the materials updated?
Yes! We regularly update our library with the latest educational resources aligned with the Minsu curriculum.
Still Have Questions?
If your question wasn't answered here, please reach out to our support team. We're always happy to help Minsu students!
